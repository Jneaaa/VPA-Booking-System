Reservation Policies

1. Eligibility

Only current students, faculty, and staff of Central Philippine University are eligible to make reservations through this system.

2. Booking Period

Reservations must be made at least 3 days in advance. Same-day bookings are not permitted.

3. Time Limitations

Each user may reserve a facility or equipment for a maximum of 4 hours per day, unless granted special approval.

4. Cancellation

Cancellations must be submitted no later than 24 hours before the reserved time. Failure to do so may result in suspension of booking privileges.

5. Accountability

Users are responsible for any damage or loss incurred during the reservation period. Misuse may lead to disciplinary action.

6. Special Requests

Requests for extended use or after-hours access must be coordinated through the Office of Property Custodians at least one week in advance.
