Reservation Policies

1. Eligibility

The equipment and facility booking services are exclusively available to currently enrolled students, active faculty members, and employed staff of Central Philippine University. Valid institutional identification will be required upon equipment pickup or facility access. External parties may request special permission through the Office of Property Custodians.

2. Booking Period

All reservation requests must be submitted at least three (3) business days prior to the intended use date. This advance notice period allows for proper scheduling and resource allocation. Emergency or exceptional circumstances may be considered at the discretion of the Property Custodian with proper justification.

3. Time Limitations

To ensure equitable access for all university members, individual users are limited to four (4) hours of reservation time per calendar day across all facilities and equipment. Extended usage requests must be submitted in writing to the Office of Property Custodians at least five (5) business days in advance, accompanied by a detailed justification.

4. Cancellation Policy

Reservations must be canceled no less than twenty-four (24) hours before the scheduled time through the official booking system. Repeated late cancellations (three or more within an academic semester) or no-shows will result in temporary suspension of booking privileges for thirty (30) calendar days. Extenuating circumstances may be appealed in writing to the Property Custodian.

5. User Accountability

The reserving party assumes full financial responsibility for any damage, loss, or excessive wear incurred during their reservation period. A pre-use inspection report must be completed for high-value equipment. Users must report any pre-existing damage immediately. Misuse of university property may result in disciplinary action under the university's code of conduct.

6. Special Accommodations

Requests for after-hours access, extended duration use, or specialized equipment configurations require written approval. Please submit these requests to the Office of Property Custodians at least seven (7) business days in advance. Approval depends on availability, demonstrated need, and institutional priorities.
